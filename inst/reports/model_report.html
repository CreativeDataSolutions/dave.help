Predictive modeling

Machine learning based predictive modelling was used to predict class. A single model was fit and optimized based on predictive performance on the test data.

A random forest model was developed on 55 samples and 188 predictors to predict 2 classes: diabetic.., non.diabetic.. with no pre-processing. Cross validation was done by resampling: cross-validated (7 fold, repeated 3 times) . Tuning parameter ‘mtry’ was held constant at a value of 62.
